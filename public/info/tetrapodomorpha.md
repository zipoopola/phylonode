The image is of Tiktaalik from around 375MYA. It is actually quite a derived example of a Tetrapodomorph, with the other more basal examples looking even more similar to Lungfish. Tiktaalik is an extremely famous transitional form between true tetrapods and their lobe-finned fish ancestors.
